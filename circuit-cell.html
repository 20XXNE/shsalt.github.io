Circuit Cell

Finished in March of 2015, Circuit Cell is my final, cumulative project in high school. It was conceived as a project to teach fifth graders about the basics of physics, specifically electricity and magnetism; using childlike and friendly graphics, interactivity, and simplcity. I used everything I had learned up to this point. Site features include interactive drag-and-drop tutorials, quizzes, full responsiveness, and galleries. You can view this project here.
